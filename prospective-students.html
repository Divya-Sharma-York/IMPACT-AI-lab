Prospective Students

Information for those interested in joining our research team

Thank you for your interest in the IMPACT-AI Lab and in the rewarding experience of doing research together. Our lab is committed to fostering a welcoming, collaborative, and respectful environment where all voices are heard and valued.

I actively encourage applications from students who identify with groups that have been historically underrepresented in the fields of artificial intelligence and health research. This includes, though is not limited to, women, Indigenous students, Black and other racialized individuals, people with disabilities, and members of LGBTQ2S+ communities.

The lab is housed in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at York University in Toronto, and works closely with healthcare partners such as the University Health Network. We focus on developing and applying cutting-edge AI methods to drive equitable, high-impact solutions in healthcare.

Due to appointments at both York university and Dalla Lana School of Public Health, University of Toronto, I invite applications from both institutes.

We are currently looking for students interested in exploring research topics such as:

  • Reinforcement learning for personalized clinical decision-making
  • Generative AI
  • Multimodal Integrative AI modeling
  • Foundation models and Agentic AI for clinical reasoning
  • Applications in Mental Health, Liver diseases, Osteoarthritis, Cancer, Genomics

    If you're excited by the intersection of AI, equity, and healthcare innovation, I’d love to hear from you.

    • PhD and Master's Positions

    If you are interested in joining our research group for a Master’s (MSc) or PhD in Mathematics & Statistics at York University, please ensure that you meet all admission requirements outlined by the Faculty of Graduate Studies as well as the Department of Mathematics & Statistics.

    To express your interest in working with me, please email divya03@yorku.ca with the following materials:

    Subject Line:
    MSc / PhD Application - [Your Full Name]

    Required Materials (PDFs preferred):

    • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
    • A short Statement of Purpose / Research Interests :
      Describe your academic background, research interests, motivation for pursuing graduate studies, and reasons for wanting to work with me.
    • English Proficiency Test Score (if applicable): IELTS or TOEFL, if English is not your first language.
    • Up to Two Relevant Publications or Project Reports (if available)
    • Names and Email Addresses of Two Referees
    • Link to your GitHub profile (or equivalent code portfolio)
    • Links or PDFs of relevant published or submitted work (if available)

    Review Process

    I will review your materials and contact shortlisted applicants for an interview. Due to the volume of applications, I may only respond to those selected for further consideration. I appreciate your understanding.

    If there is a strong match, you will be invited to submit a formal application to the Department of Mathematics and Statistics via the Faculty of Graduate Studies application portal. Your referees will then be asked to submit letters of recommendation.

    Applications are welcome year-round. Early contact is encouraged for scholarship consideration and planning.

    Undergraduate Research Opportunities

    If you are an undergraduate student at the York University interested in machine learning and healthcare, you are welcome to reach out about potential research opportunities in our lab.

    Please email me directly at divya03@yorku.ca with the following:

    • Your CV
    • Your most recent transcript (unofficial is fine)
    • A brief write-up (2–3 paragraphs) describing:
      • Your interest in machine learning and/or healthcare applications
      • Specific topics or problems you’re curious to explore
      • Your prior coursework or experience relevant to this interest

    Postdoctoral Positions

    We are always excited to hear from researchers passionate about the intersection of machine learning and healthcare.

    If you are inquiring about postdoctoral opportunities, please email me at divya03@yorku.ca with the subject line: Postdoc Inquiry – [Your Name], and include the following materials:

    • Your CV
    • A short statement describing:
      • Why you'd like to work with me
      • Your current research interests and how they align with our lab
      • Any relevant experience in machine learning, healthcare, or related fields
    • Link to your GitHub profile (or equivalent code portfolio)
    • Links or PDFs of relevant published or submitted work
